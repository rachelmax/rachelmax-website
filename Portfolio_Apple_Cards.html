iOS Cards Application

I spent six years at Apple in the Consumer Apps Group from 2007 until 2013. The Cards App was the first iOS project I worked on so it holds a special place in my heart. The app allowed you to easily customize letterpress cards and then mail them using the contacts on your phone.
My Role

I worked on the UX, Interaction Design of the theme browser, transitions, the edit states of card and envelope, a portion of the UI, designed various elements, and worked closely with our engineering team in the role ofart director to make sure the shipping build looked and felt right.
