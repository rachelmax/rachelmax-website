on Radio Valencia

I had a radio show for two years from late 2013 until Nov 2015 on Radio Valencia in San Francisco. The MaxMix was an electic two hour weekly show and my goal was to never play the same song twice. I didn't quite succeed but I got close. Most of the shows had themes but some were just mixes of music I was feeling that day.
