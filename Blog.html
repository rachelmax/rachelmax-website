Computational Arts Research & Theory Blog

This blog documents research, projects, and reflections during my 2019 MA in Computational Arts at Goldsmiths University of London.

My artwork falls in the category of critical and speculative design as I use humor and satire to challenge sociocultural norms, ethical implications of technology, and conventional approaches to designing human object interactions. My work differs from critical and speculative design in that work is fully functional and interactive - existing in the present.
