Branded Navbar Animations

On special occasions and as Houzz launched in new areas, animated logos were added to the navbar. These gifs were for St. Patrick's Day, July 4th, the World Cup, Valentine's Day, the UK launch, the Australia launch, Halloween, New Year's Eve, Thanksgiving, the Swedish launch, and the Winter holidays.
My Role

I designed these gifs in Illustrator, animated them in After Efffects and Cinema 4D, and compressed them using ImageOptim. The Gifs feature an older Houzz logo.
